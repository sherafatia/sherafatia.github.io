Frequently asked questions

Who is Anahid Research Academy for?

Motivated middle school, high school, and early undergraduate students who want to learn by doing, build a real portfolio, and—at the Scholar level—publish openly and compete in fairs like STS/ISEF/JSHS.

What outcomes do students graduate with?

Explorer: mini report/poster. Innovator: Preprint draft + abstract + Demo Day talk. Scholar: GitHub repo, OSF preprint, competition packet (poster/abstract/slides), and optional submission to JEI/JSR‑HS.

What areas can I study?

Data Science, Neuroscience, Computational Neuroscience, AI/LLMs, Neuroimaging (fMRI/EEG/fNIRS), Neurotechnology/BCI (safe consumer hardware), Medicine/Digital Health, Bioinformatics, Cognitive Science.

How are projects scoped for feasibility and safety?

We use public/open datasets, laptop‑friendly compute, and clear weekly milestones. No human/animal data collection by students. Parent consent and privacy safeguards are required.
