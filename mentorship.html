Mentorship Program

I am passionate about sharing my love for scientific discovery with the next generation of scientists through personalized mentorship in computational neuroscience, AI, and data science.

Who is this for?

Middle school, high school, and college students with a curiosity about how the brain and AI models work and a desire to perform hands-on cutting edge research. Each student works on a graduate-level research project tailored to their interests and skill level, gaining real-world experience in coding, data analysis, and scientific thinking.

What students learn?

  • How to analyze real neuroscience or AI datasets
  • How to use Python, Jupyter Notebooks, and key tools
  • How to design and publish a scientific research paper

How to apply

If you're interested, fill out the contact form with a short paragraph about your background and interests for a free consultation.

Besides my program, I am also a research mentor at Lumiere Education, Mehta+, and Superprof.
